Housing project

FABI Real Estate is proud to be part of Saudi Arabia's housing initiative with "Saudia Heights." This meticulously planned residential community offers diverse housing options, from villas to apartments, prioritizing accessibility and sustainability. With advanced infrastructure and smart features, Saudia Heights embodies our commitment to quality and innovation, contributing to the Kingdom's vision for affordable, inclusive housing solutions.

Kingdom Tower

Kingdom Tower by FABI Real Estate is a prestigious project in the heart of the city, offering luxurious offices, retail spaces, and upscale residential apartments. It embodies FABI's commitment to excellence, showcasing innovation and quality in every aspect of its design and construction.

Burj Khalifa

Khalifa Tower, developed by FABI Real Estate, is an iconic skyscraper that redefines urban living. Rising majestically in a prime location, it offers a harmonious blend of prestigious office spaces, exclusive retail outlets, and opulent residential apartments. Khalifa Tower stands as a testament to FABI's unwavering commitment to excellence and innovation, setting new standards in luxury and sophistication.
